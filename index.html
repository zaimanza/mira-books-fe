History

Colombia Asia is an local private hospital that was founded in 2017. It started its first hospital in Johor Bahru in 2017. Now, Colombia Asia provide 5 hospitals in Johor, Penang and Kuala Lumpur.

Colombia Asia offers efficient and affordable medical services through its hospitals with modern technology and professional doctors and nurses, these hospitals located nearby residential areas, making quality private hospital more accessible patients. This private hospital complete with various types of facility and different types of room for the convenient of out patients. This private hospital also is a panel for more than 70 insurance companies.
